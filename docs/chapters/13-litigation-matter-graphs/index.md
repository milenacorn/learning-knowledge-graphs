# Litigation and Matter Knowledge Graphs

## Summary

This chapter applies knowledge graph techniques to litigation and matter
management: matter graphs, legal document graphs, legal entity resolution,
conflicts checking, and case law graphs built from courts, jurisdictions,
holdings, and precedent. It covers the full litigation lifecycle — parties,
filings, docket entries, and timelines — through to e-discovery (litigation
holds, document review, privilege logs), and is anchored by a worked example
built from a real, publicly-documented litigation matter using only public
filings and court records. Readers will finish able to model a litigation
matter as a knowledge graph and explain why legal entity resolution and
conflicts checking are graph problems, not just database lookups.
