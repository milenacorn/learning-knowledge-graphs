# Governance and Maintenance

## Summary

This chapter covers the ongoing work of keeping a knowledge graph accurate
and trustworthy over time: data governance, provenance, and quality,
followed by graph versioning, schema evolution, and data lineage. It closes
with access control, change management, audit trails, and update cadence —
the operational practices that turn a one-time knowledge graph project into
a maintained, living system. Readers will finish able to draft a governance
plan for a knowledge graph they've built, covering who can change what and
how changes are tracked over time.
