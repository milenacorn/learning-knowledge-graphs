# Contracts, IP, and Legal Standards

## Summary

This chapter covers the transactional and standards side of legal knowledge
graphs: contract graphs and clause libraries, obligation tracking, expertise
location, client relationship graphs, and outside-counsel and regulatory
graphs. It also covers intellectual property — patent citation graphs, prior
art networks, and IP portfolio graphs — and closes with SALI and FOLIO, two
real, industry-adopted legal ontologies that formalize much of the
vocabulary introduced across both legal chapters. Readers will finish able
to model a contract's obligations as a graph and locate where a real-world
legal ontology like FOLIO would plug into their own schema.
