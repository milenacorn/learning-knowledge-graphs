# Cross-Industry Applications

## Summary

This chapter surveys real-world knowledge graphs across search, biomedical
research, recommendation systems, product and social graphs, fraud
detection, and supply chains — including the Google Knowledge Graph as a
widely recognized example. It closes with graph visualization as the
practical bridge between a graph's internal structure and how people
actually explore it. Readers will finish able to recognize a knowledge
graph application in the wild and explain what problem it's solving.
